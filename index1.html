About me

How I got started in Computer Science

  While I was in high school, I enrolled in the Running Start program for my Junior and Senior year. Basically, it gave me two free years of college. In my area it was being offered at Olympic College in Bremerton, Washington, which is where I started my higher education.

  I was at a point in my life where I wanted to find something that I could turn into a career. I had many hobbies and interests, but there wasn't anything that really jumped out at me. Writing was something I enjoyed doing, though I didn't see it as a profession to pursue.

  I took some different classes in varying fields, but nothing was sticking. Eventually, a friend of mine that I played football with suggested I take the Programming Concepts course at OC. I registered the following quarter, went through the whole thing, and loved it. I still remember the professor, Mr. Dondi Hanson. I never met him in person, but he was a good teacher.

  He started us with learning PHP. Not for using it in the normal sense, but for understanding the core principles of programming, hence the title of the course. We did some very simple assignments that taught us the basics of conditions, loops, variables, etc. It blew my mind that I was able to take a huge file filled with raw data (not so raw in that case, but it was cool nonetheless) and be able to turn it into readable logs that documented the trends in the information.

  It set me on my path. After that class, I started taking some more programming classes. A little bit of web development, XML, Intro to Computing type of stuff. Once I graduated from high school and enrolled in the University of Washington Tacoma I dove into their Computer Science and Systems program. What a wild ride it's been. Suffice it to say, I've done a lot of everything.

  During my time at UWT I've built all kinds of projects:

    • Java Applications
    • Computer Architecture Simulations
    • PostgreSQL and MySQL Servers
    • Responsive Websites
    • Mobile Android Apps
    • Web Services
    • Video Games
      • Unity3D
      • Web-based

  I do freelance work of all kinds. Check out my Portfolio to see some of my past work and send me an email if there's anything I can do for you.
